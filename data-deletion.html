Data Deletion Instructions

How to Request Data Deletion

We respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete your data from Bortoana, you can follow these steps:

Option 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Log into your Bortoana account
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select "Privacy & Data"
  4. Click on "Delete My Data"
  5. Confirm your choice

Option 2: Email Request

You can also send a data deletion request to our support team:

  1. Send an email to [Your Support Email]
  2. Subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include your account information
  4. We will process your request within 30 days

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon your request, we will delete:

  • Your account information
  • Your uploaded content
  • Your activity history
  • Any stored preferences

Timeline

We will process your deletion request within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about the data deletion process, please contact us at [Your Support Email].

Facebook Data

If you used Facebook to log in to our app, you can also remove our app's access to your Facebook data through your Facebook settings:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings
  2. Select "Apps and Websites"
  3. Find Bortoana in the list
  4. Remove the app
